Our Story

Founded in 2015, Saffron Spoon began with a simple vision: to bring authentic Indian flavors and royal dining experiences to food lovers. What started as a small family-run kitchen has now become one of the city's most beloved restaurants.

Our chefs combine traditional recipes passed down through generations with modern techniques, using only the freshest locally-sourced ingredients. Every dish tells a story of passion, creativity, and commitment to culinary excellence.
